Introduction

About Me

I am an Entertainment Management student at the University of Central Florida's Rosen College of Hospitality Management with a minor in Digital Media. My interests sit at the intersection of entertainment, marketing, live events, music, tourism, and digital storytelling.

Throughout college, I have gained experience in music, tourism, event production, advertising, and project management. I also founded Knights Records, UCF's first student-run mock record label, where I lead teams focused on artist development, marketing, branding, A&R, and event planning.

My career goal is to work in entertainment marketing and create strategies that help artists, brands, and experiences connect with audiences in meaningful ways.
